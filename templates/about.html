The Problem

Traditional irrigation systems often waste water through inefficient watering schedules and lack of real-time environmental data. This leads to overwatering, increased utility costs, and environmental strain.

Our Solution

WaterIT leverages AI and IoT technology to create a smart watering system that adapts to real-time conditions. This IoT-based technology waters the plants only when necessary. By analyzing soil moisture, weather forecasts, and plant needs, we optimize plant health.
